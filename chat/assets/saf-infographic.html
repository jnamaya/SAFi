SAF

Closed-Loop System

1. Values

Function: Serve as the ethical foundation and guide decision-making.

Role: Provide the basis for what is right.

2. Intellect

Function: The reasoning faculty that evaluates choices based on values.

Role: Bridge between values and action.

3. Will

Function: The driving force that translates decisions into action.

Role: Actualizes alignment.

4. Conscience

Function: Provides real-time internal feedback on alignment.

Role: Enables self-correction.

5. Spirit

Function: Integrates the system and perceives the long-term ethical arc.

Role: Reflects long-term integrity.
