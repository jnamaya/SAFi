What SAFi Does

SAFi gives you control over your AI systems by enforcing four key principles:

  • Value Sovereignty – You decide the values, not the model.
  • Full Traceability – Every decision is transparent and auditable.
  • Model Independence – Swap AI models without losing your identity.
  • Long-Term Consistency – Maintain alignment over time.
